What This App Does

📊 Live Trip Profitability

Every Trip Expense (fuel, posho, fines, tolls) rolls up to the Freight Trip instantly. See Profit & Margin while the truck is still on the road — no end-of-month surprises.

💰 Full Accounting Chain

Submit a trip → Sales Invoice, Purchase Invoice, Journal Entries and Payment Entries are created automatically. Everything posts to the General Ledger.

⛽ Anti-Theft & Cost Control

Route benchmarks flag excess fuel draw and inflated allowances in real time. Catch siphoning and over-claims before the money is gone.

🚛 Owned or Hired — Same System

Trucks can be Owned or Hired. Drivers can be Employees or Contractors. The system handles the payment differently for each — automatically.

⚙️ Logistics Settings

The bridge to your Chart of Accounts. Maps every logistics concept (fuel, allowance, fines, income, bank) to a ledger account. Auto-filled from company defaults on install. Enable Auto-create accounting for full automation.

Daily Workflow — 4 Simple Steps

1Create Waybill — record what's being carried: customer, cargo, weight, loading/offloading points, POD.

2Create Freight Trip — pick Route + Truck + Driver + Waybill. Set the Agreed Rate. Revenue auto-calculates.

3Log Trip Expenses — each fuel stop, posho, fine, toll, or repair. Submit each → Journal Entry posts automatically.

4Submit the Freight Trip — review the live P&L, then Submit. Accounting entries are created automatically: Sales Invoice (revenue), Purchase Invoice (hired truck), Payment Entry (hired driver).

🛡️ Anti-Theft & Cost Control

Benchmarks on each route become automatic trip audits

⛽ Fuel Theft Detection

The route defines a Standard Fuel Allocation. If the trip draws more, you see a red warning with the exact liter variance:

Fuel Variance = Actual Drawn − Standard

💵 Allowance Control

The route defines a Standard Driver Allowance. If the trip claims more posho, you're warned before approving.

📉 Negative Margin Alert

If a trip is losing money (Profit Margin < 0%), the system warns you in red at save time.

📋 Full Audit Trail

Every expense has a receipt attachment. Every change is tracked. Every shilling is in the GL.
